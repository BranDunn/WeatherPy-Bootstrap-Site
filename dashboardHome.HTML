The goal is to analyze the correlation of latitudinal position with weather. More than 500 world cities have been randomly selected programatically. Weather data for each city was then queried via API, and analyzed to determine relationships between latitude and four targeted weather outcomes: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.
